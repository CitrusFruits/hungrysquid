About

Hungry Squid is a hackathon project developed at Skuid, originally designed to be an easter egg in the product. Since the game never made its way into the product itself, I have made it available here for anyone and everyone to enjoy.

The game itself is a play on Skuid's product. In Skuid the product, dragging and dropping are a big part of the product, so I chose to make it a big part of the gameplay in Hungry Squid. The taco isn't of any particular significance, but hey, who doesn't like tacos?

The squid, taco, and fire sprites were generously made by Tyrick Christian. The graphical tiling patterns were adapted from some open source images found online, and I apologize for not remembering where they were from or who made them. The gameplay design and development was done by me, Andrew Duensing.
